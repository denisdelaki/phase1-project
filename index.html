Vehicle Punctures

Incase of a Puncture:

  1. Find the Leak
  2. Loosen the Lug Nuts and Jack Up the car
  3. Take Off the Nuts and clean the Hole
  4. Insert the Plug
  5. Inflate the Tire

Braking System Failures

Incase Brake system Failure Do these:

  1. First, Downshift to a lower Gear
  2. If the Car has regular brakes, Pump the brake pedal fast and hard to build up the brake Fluid pressure for 3 to 4 pumps
  3. If the problem persist, apply the parking brake and be ready for the car to skid
  4. If it persist put the car into a low gear and steer in a safe direction until the car completely stop

Engine failures

Common Engine Problems and possible solution:

  1. Failing Oil Pump: Use oil with the right viscosity for easy flow
  2. Prolonged Engine Detonation: Indicated by Knocking Noise
  3. Engine will not Start. This could be due to battery related problem. Jump-start the battery. It could be due to starter or clogged fuel filter

Clutch Complications

Common Clutch Problems

  1. Clutch not allowing to release: This is due to worn clutch pedal busing and excess oil in transmission
  2. Clutch Slipping
  3. Clutch Noise: Caused by worn and misaligned bearings, stop pins, and loose flywheel bolts
